NR ExpressRail

ExpressRail is our division managing passenger railroads and highspeed trains. Our ExpressRail fleet consists of famous rolling stock like the TGV Duplex and Talgo. Our trains operate at a top speed of 350 kilometres per hour. ExpressRail is committed towards creating unforgettable experiences that blend elegance, efficiency and innovation.


NR CargoRail

CargoRail is our division managing and operating freight railroads and freight trains. Our CargoRail locomotive fleet consists of powerful and reliable locomotives from Alstom, EMD and General Electric that pull the heaviest of our freight trains with ease. Our freight trains operate in dedicated freight corridors ensuring faster and safer movement of your cargo.
